Pattern Recognition for Financial Time Series Forecasting

Time-frequency signal processing and deep learning for stock price prediction

Python TensorFlow STFT CNN Yahoo Finance

Objective

Problem

Financial time series like stock prices are non-stationary signals. Traditional models fail to capture time and frequency patterns simultaneously.

Approach

Stock prices are treated as signals. Short-Time Fourier Transform (STFT) converts them into spectrograms, which a CNN model uses to predict future prices.
